History

Rafting has its origins in the 19th century, when explorers and settlers in North America used makeshift rafts to navigate whitewater rivers rapids. The sport gained popularity in the mid-20th century, with the introduction of inflatable rafts and the establishment of the first commercial rafting companies. Today, whitewater rafting is enjoyed by millions of people around the world, from casual day-trippers to seasoned adventurers seeking extreme thrills.
